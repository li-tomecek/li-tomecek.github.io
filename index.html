Robot Runner

A small-scale Unreal Engine university project designed to implement learned AI techniques. Robot Runner uses the AI Director concept to dynamically adjust the difficulty of this endless runner.
Developed as a team of three, where each member took an active role in the entire design and implementation process. Using in-game parameters such as player health, a simple mathematical model is used to calculate the game's "intensity". The difficulty is raised if this intensty remains low and is lowered after spikes of high intensity.

Ongoing Projects

Unreal "RPG Roguelike"

A personal project intended to familiarize myself with Unreal Engine and visual scripting with blueprints.
Building on the intended concept of 'Apex of the Arcane', the player will control a cast of characters with RPG-inspired classes as they traverse through a series or rooms, each beginning with a turn-based battle. Cleared rooms will then rewards players with additional bonuses such as permanent stat buffs, new abilities, or upgrades to existing abilities.
As of now, the focus of development has been on creating a basic combat sequence with a unique action for each class type, a 'BP' management system for casting spells and taking actions, as well as developing a base for the enemies' AI model for action and target selection. Units are assigned bright colours for debugging purposes. Next steps involve asset creation and animations for both characters and environment, the addition of enemy types, and level switching as the player progresses through rooms.

Apex of the Arcane

A 2D top-down personal project intended to familiarize myself with Unity and C# scripting.
The player will control a cast of wizards as they traverse through a series of rooms in an arcane tower, each containing a turn-based strategy minigame.
At present, gameplay is limited to a basic combat sequence, with enemies mathematically modeling each of their combat options during their turn.
