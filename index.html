Robot Runner

A small-scale Unreal Engine university project designed to implement learned AI techniques. Robot Runner uses the AI Director concept to dynamically adjust the difficulty of this endless runner.

Developed as a team of three, where each member took an active role in the entire design and implementation process. Using in-game parameters such as player health, a simple mathematical model is used to calculate the game's "intensity". The difficulty is raised if this intensty remains low and is lowered after spikes of high intensity.

Spell Masters

A magical rock-paper-scissors battle created as a solo term project. This project was used to practice programming design patters, as well as UI design.

In the preparation phase, choose which spells you would like to use and apply any cards to your sigils for additional effects! You and your opposing AI will simultaneously reveal your chosen spells in the resolution phase.

Art & Smaller Projects

The Acolyte

An original character modeled in ZBrush, retopologized in Maya, and painted in Substance Painter.
The Acolyte is a gargoyle of a gothic cathedral turned into a living protector of its crypts.
This was created as a course project and is my first time using this pipeline. Retopology took the model from over 9M tris to 40K.
